About Us


ASK Engineering concept took shape as a result of our objective, Indian manufacturing industry which is at a threshold of becoming a global player, has to adopt to the modern and ever changing tool design and manufacturing concept using latest CAD/CAM technology.

ASK Engineering world, which is a one stop answer for tool making. We can provide you with total solution for your tool making plans. To achieve this we have the latest CAD/CAM packages such as Ideas / UG and a team of professionals to work on it.

While design is our strongest area of interest, never the less we are equally competent to give shape to our design through manufacturing. In one sentence, give us the component drawings and we will give you the Tooling Models or Ready To Use Tooling.
